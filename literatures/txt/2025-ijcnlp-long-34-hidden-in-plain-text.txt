C.1.1    Results with additional datasets and

We ran the ICRL method with GPT-4o as the col-
luding teams model on the Uni dataset. It only
achieved a task success rate of 0.58, not more
than random chance. We also ran the GBRL
method on a version of the Gender dataset called
Gender-Easy that had a Max-Clean-Success of 0.5
and where the only predictive feature was the gen-
der of the applicant. Here, all three seeds converged
to bypass the overseer and to obtain a task success
rate of 100%.
